The human eye can recognize or differentiate around 10 million distinct colors. A computer screen can generate 16 to the 6th power or approximated 17 million different colors. As a web master, you can use hexidecimal notation for your colors or generic names (red, green, light green, etc.) for the colors. You can code colors either way. An example would be #d92626. The "#" sign is used to code hexadecimal. You could also use font color= "lightgreen" with the appropriate tags.
